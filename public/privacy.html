Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

ForexTradeFi ("we", "us", "the site") operates forextradefi.com. This page explains what data we handle and how. We keep it minimal — we are an education-first site, not a data business.

1. Information we collect

  • Email address — only if you choose to join our newsletter. It is stored to send you gold-market updates and nothing else.
  • Trading journal data — if you sign in to the free journal, your entries are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) under your account. They are private to you.
  • Account info via Google Sign-In — if you use it, we receive your basic Google profile (name, email, avatar) to create your journal account.
  • Usage data — standard, non-identifying analytics and server logs (pages viewed, approximate region, device type).

2. Cookies & advertising

We may display ads through Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party cookies at aboutads.info.

3. Affiliate links

Some links on this site — for example on the Brokers page — are affiliate or referral links. If you open an account through them, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. We only list firms and tools we use or would use ourselves. This is educational content, not financial advice.

4. How we use your data

  • To send the newsletter you asked for (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • To provide and secure the free trading journal.
  • To understand which content is useful and improve the site.
  • To show relevant advertising through Google AdSense.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Third-party services

The site loads data and tools from third parties, each with their own privacy policies: Google (Firebase, Sign-In, AdSense), TradingView, CoinGecko, frankfurter.app, alternative.me, Finnhub and Gumroad.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to access or delete your email or journal data at any time. To leave the newsletter, use the unsubscribe option or contact us. To delete journal data, remove entries in the app or request account deletion.

7. Children

This site is not directed to anyone under 18. Trading is for adults only.
